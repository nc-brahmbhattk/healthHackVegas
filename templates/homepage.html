Drinki

Personalized Health Recommendations and Nutrition Planning

OUR MISSION


Drinki is a multi-objective optimization learning platform which aims to enable and empower health improvement by combining genomics and activity tracking.

How it works


Drinki uses genomics to understand various risks an individual might be undergoing and applies that learning to calibrate the model. It goes even further to predict and prevent individuals high at risk of severe health conditions by tracking and monitoring heart rate patterns, irregularities in sleep cycles, and correlation between calorie intake vs. sleep.
